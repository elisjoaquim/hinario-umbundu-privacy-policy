Privacy Policy for Hinário Umbundu App

Effective Date: 2025-03-11

1. Introduction

At Hinário Umbundu, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Hinário Umbundu, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Users can create an account using email and password or through third-party authentication providers such as Google, Facebook, and Apple. We collect necessary authentication details, including usernames, email addresses, and profile photos (when available), to manage user accounts.
  • User-Generated Content: Any data that users generate within the app, including comments on hymns, reactions, and other content users create.
  • Account Preferences: Your settings and preferences.
  • Location Data: Access to your device’s location for location-based advertisements and app improvements.
  • Usage Data & Analytics: Collected through Firebase Analytics and Facebook Analytics to analyze app interactions and performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used for:

  • Enabling user authentication and login via email/password or third-party providers (Google, Facebook, Apple).
  • Allowing users to generate and interact with content.
  • Providing location-based advertisements.
  • Analyzing app performance and user engagement.
  • Maintaining and improving the app's functionality.

4. Data Storage & Security

All collected data is securely stored in Firebase, which follows industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Children’s Privacy

Hinário Umbundu is child-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information beyond necessary login and user-generated content. If a parent believes their child has provided additional personal data, they may contact us for deletion.

6. Account Deletion

Users can delete their accounts at any time. Upon deletion:

  • All personal data, including login credentials, profile photos, and user-generated content, will be permanently removed.
  • This process is irreversible.

To delete your account, visit the account settings in the app or contact support at support@hinarioumbundu.com.

7. Third-Party Services & Advertising

Hinário Umbundu uses third-party services like Firebase for functionality and advertising partners for personalized ads based on location. Users can disable location tracking in their device settings.

8. Analytics & Tracking

We use Firebase Analytics and Facebook Analytics to track user interactions, including:

  • Pages and features accessed.
  • Session duration.
  • Device information (e.g., model, OS).
  • General location (not precise GPS tracking).

This data helps us improve the app experience. Users can manage analytics preferences in their device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the app, and we encourage users to review them.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at:

support@hinarioumbundu.com
